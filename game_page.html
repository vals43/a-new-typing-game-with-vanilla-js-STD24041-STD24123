Typing Game

Welcome to the typing game!

Improve your speed and accuracy by typing words within a time limit. Ready to take on the challenge and become the fastest?

Choose your game mode

  • Difficulty level: Easy, Medium, Hard
  • Language: Choose between English, French, or Spanish.
  • Timed mode: 10s, 15s, 20s, etc. for more challenge!
  • Number of words: Select how many words to type (10, 20, 30...)
  • Theme: Choose from the available themes

How to play

Read the displayed words and type them in the field at the bottom of the screen. Be fast and accurate to maximize your score!

Statistics and results

Once your game is over, you'll see your WPM (words per minute), accuracy, and progress!

Tips to improve your score

  • Practice regularly to increase your speed.
  • Don't focus only on speed – accuracy is key!
  • Timed mode will let you test your speed under pressure.
